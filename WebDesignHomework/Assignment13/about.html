ABOUT

Berserk is a dark fantasy manga series that follows the journey of Guts, a lone mercenary with a tragic past, and his complicated relationships with those around him. Created by Kentaro Miura, the story explores themes of ambition, destiny, and survival amidst a brutal, war-torn world. Through Guts' struggles and triumphs, Berserk becomes a story of resilience and the human spirit, pushing the boundaries of epic storytelling in graphic novels.
