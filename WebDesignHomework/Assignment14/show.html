Explore the Berserk Anime

The Berserk anime adaptation of 1997 captured the dark and gritty essence of the manga, bringing the world of Guts, Griffith, and Casca to life. With its haunting soundtrack and unforgettable visuals, it remains a fan favorite to this day.
