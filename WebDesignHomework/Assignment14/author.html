About The Author

Kentaro Miura

Kentaro Miura (1966â€“2021) was a legendary manga artist and the creator of the critically acclaimed dark fantasy series Berserk. Born in Chiba, Japan, Miura began drawing at a young age, demonstrating exceptional artistic talent. His career skyrocketed with the publication of *Berserk* in 1989, a series that would go on to influence countless creators and fans worldwide.

Known for his intricate artwork, deep storytelling, and exploration of complex themes, Miura crafted a tale of ambition, sacrifice, and resilience through the journey of Guts, the Black Swordsman. His ability to balance brutal action with heartfelt emotion set him apart as one of the greatest storytellers in manga history.

Despite his immense success, Miura remained dedicated to his craft until his passing in 2021. His legacy lives on through his masterpiece and the countless lives he touched with his work.
