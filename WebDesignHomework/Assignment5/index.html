Retail Website

One idea for a website is to create a retail website. This would include images for products, use of buttons to purchase and for going to other tabs on the website.
The website tone would be dependent on the product. Creating a website for relaxing products such as candles would envoke a calm tone. Encorporating repition will also be key to create a calming tone, with color decision being a huge factor on establishing the correct tone.

Community Board Website

Another idea is to create a community board for other users to talk on topics of their choosing. Users should also be able to create their own accounts and create posts for other users to comment on.
The tone that would fit this type of sit would be a playful tone to envoke more friendly and light conversations about topics.

Photography and Art Website

Users should be able to create their own art profiles and display their art as a showcase. Users should also be able to enter in competitions to have the winners art displayed on the homepage. This should create a platform for up and coming artists to showcase their talent and compete in fun competitions.
The tone of this website would be a playful tone to encourage fun competition, and a fun website atmosphere.
