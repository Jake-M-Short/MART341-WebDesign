How To Make The Perfect Cup Of Drip Coffee

To make this cup of coffee you will need a drip coffee machine.

Step 1: Grind your coffee beans to a table salt consistency.


Step 2: Pour clean water into the drip coffee machine.


Step 3: Turn the machine off right after it finishes brewing to avoid a burnt taste and serve.
